# Privacy and data minimisation

## Purpose

This document records technical decisions related to privacy, data minimisation and security.

## Initial principles

- Do not collect unnecessary personal data.
- Do not add tracking by default.
- Do not commit secrets, credentials or private configuration values.
- Limit administrative access to authorised users.
- Treat privacy and security requirements as part of the application design.

## Technical areas to consider

- authentication
- admin access
- contact forms
- volunteer or participation forms
- stored personal data
- uploaded files
- logging
- analytics
- cookies
- backups and retention

## Notes

This document should record technical implementation decisions and constraints.

Legal wording, policy text and organisational approval should be handled through the appropriate external product or governance documents.
