ReduceCO2Now aims to operate this website in a privacy-friendly and data-minimising way.

We do not use analytics, advertising cookies, tracking pixels or profiling tools to monitor how visitors use this website.

We do not create user accounts for normal visitors and we do not try to identify visitors by their behaviour on the site.

What personal data do we collect?

We aim not to collect personal data about your use of this website. In particular, we do not use analytics, advertising pixels, behavioural tracking or profiling tools.

However, when you visit any website, some technical information may be processed automatically so that the page can be delivered to your browser. Depending on the hosting provider used for the live site, this may include:

  • the page or file requested;
  • the date and time of the request;
  • your browser type and version;
  • your operating system;
  • the referring page, if any;
  • your IP address.

The exact technical access data and retention period must be confirmed once the final hosting provider is chosen.

Why may technical access data be processed?

Technical access data may be processed only where necessary to deliver the website, maintain security, diagnose technical problems or prevent misuse.

Where such processing is necessary for the secure and reliable operation of the website, the legal basis is normally our legitimate interest in providing a secure, functional and reliable website.

Cookies and browser storage

This website does not use analytics, advertising or tracking cookies.

The only cookie currently planned for this website is an optional first-party preference cookie connected with the privacy notice.

If you choose “Remember this choice” in the privacy notice, we store one small cookie in your browser for 180 days. This cookie only records that the privacy notice has been shown. It does not identify you, is not used for analytics, and is not shared with third parties.

If you choose to continue without saving, this cookie is not stored and the privacy notice may appear again on a later visit.

After 180 days, the cookie expires automatically and the privacy notice may be shown again.

External links and external content

This website may link to external platforms such as YouTube, Discord, LinkedIn, Reddit, TikTok or Instagram.

We aim not to load external videos, social media widgets or other third-party tracking content automatically. If you choose to open an external platform or play external content, that service may process data under its own privacy rules.

We do not receive tracking information from those platforms simply because you read this page.

Newsletter, contact forms and participation forms

If this website later offers a newsletter signup, contact form, volunteer form or participation form, we will update this privacy policy before those features go live.

No newsletter, contact or participation data should be collected through this website unless you actively provide it.

If a third-party newsletter service is used, the provider, purpose, legal basis, data categories, retention period and unsubscribe options must be described here before the signup form is published.

Do we share personal data?

We do not sell personal data.

We do not share visitor data for advertising, behavioural tracking or profiling purposes.

Technical service providers, such as hosting providers, may process data only where necessary to provide their services. Details of the live hosting provider and any other processors must be added here once they are confirmed.

How long do we keep data?

The optional privacy-notice cookie expires after 180 days.

Retention periods for server logs must be confirmed with the final hosting provider and added here before public release.

If contact forms, newsletter signups or participation forms are added later, the retention periods for that data must also be described here.

Your rights

Depending on the circumstances, you may have the right to request access to personal data concerning you, correction of inaccurate data, deletion of data, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection to processing.

If processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority.

No automated decision-making

We do not use this website for automated decision-making or profiling of visitors.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy if the website changes, if new features are added, or if the hosting or service-provider setup changes.
