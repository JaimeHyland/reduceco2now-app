# AI context: working style

The developer prefers pragmatic, maintainable full-stack development.

Important preferences:

- Answer the direct question first.
- Avoid premature technical decisions.
- Distinguish necessary work from optional or premature work.
- Keep security, privacy, accessibility and localization in mind.
- Prefer small, targeted steps.
- Document technical decisions as they are made.
- Do not duplicate product-owner, Scrum or Kanban material in repository Markdown.
